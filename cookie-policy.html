Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on kcedynamics.com, and how to control them.

Last updated: 25 June 2026

1. What this policy covers

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies and similar storage technologies are, which ones kcedynamics.com uses, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences — including for visitors in the European Union and European Economic Area, where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive require us to obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include pixels, tags, and local/session storage in your browser. They can be used to remember your preferences, recognise repeat visits, and measure how a site is used.

3. How we use cookies

When you first visit kcedynamics.com, a consent banner asks you to choose which categories of cookies you allow. Nothing outside the "Necessary" category runs until you say yes — this is enforced through Google's Consent Mode, which keeps analytics and advertising tags in a denied state until you grant permission.

Necessary

We do not currently set any strictly-necessary cookies of our own. Your cookie choice itself is remembered using your browser's local storage (not a cookie) so we don't have to ask you again on every page.

Analytics

If you opt in, we use Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) to understand how visitors use the site — pages viewed, time on page, and general traffic sources. This sets cookies such as:

CookiePurposeTypical duration
_gaDistinguishes unique visitorsUp to 2 years
_ga_*Persists session state for Google Analytics 4Up to 2 years

Data is processed by Google. See Google's own Privacy Policy for how they handle it.

Marketing

We don't currently run paid advertising campaigns that set marketing cookies. This category exists in our consent banner so that if we do introduce ad campaigns (e.g. Google Ads remarketing) in future, your existing preference is already respected.

4. Managing your preferences

You can change your choice at any time using the Cookie Settings link in the footer of any page. You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings — see your browser's help pages for instructions, though this may affect how some third-party embeds behave.

5. Legal basis

For visitors in the EU/EEA and UK, non-essential cookies (analytics and marketing) are only set with your consent, in line with the ePrivacy Directive and Article 6(1)(a) GDPR. You can withdraw consent at any time with no effect on the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data, to object to processing, to request data portability, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Contact us

Questions about this policy or how we handle your data can be sent to kwame@kcedynamics.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our use of cookies changes. Material changes will reset the consent banner so you're asked again.

This page is provided as general information about our cookie practices and is not legal advice. We recommend KCE Dynamics has it reviewed by a qualified professional for your specific regulatory obligations.
