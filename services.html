Core Services

What We Deliver

KCE Dynamics provides technical engineering services to clients building in Ghana. We cover the full project lifecycle — from first site reading through final handover.

Design & Development

We produce engineering designs for residential buildings, drainage infrastructure, and retaining structures — all backed by documented, reviewable calculations specific to the site and load conditions.

Our designs are not templates. Every project begins with site data collection, geotechnical assessment where required, and a thorough brief review before any drawing or calculation is produced.

  • Residential building structural design
  • Drainage system design and hydraulic calculations
  • Retaining structure design with slope stability analysis
  • Construction drawings and technical specifications
  • Feasibility and site investigation reports

Construction Supervision

On-site oversight from mobilisation through to practical completion. We provide systematic quality control so that non-conformances are caught in the field — not discovered in the defects report after handover.

Our supervision includes daily site diaries, materials verification, workmanship checks, and structured progress reporting to the client.

  • Daily site oversight and quality inspection
  • Materials and workmanship verification
  • Non-conformance identification and resolution
  • Progress reporting and site diaries
  • Contractor management and schedule monitoring
  • Handover inspection and snag management
Request This Service

Technical Analysis & Reporting

We convert project data into actionable intelligence. Site readings, feasibility data, structural assessments — all translated into clear technical reports that enable better decisions and protect your investment.

  • Site investigation and reading reports
  • Feasibility assessments and cost modelling
  • Structural assessment reports
  • Project progress and performance reporting
  • Risk identification and mitigation recommendations

Project & Supply Chain Optimisation

Construction delays in Ghana are often supply-chain problems. We manage and optimise procurement, delivery, and materials logistics — ensuring materials arrive on schedule, at cost, and to specification.

  • Procurement planning and supplier vetting
  • Materials scheduling and delivery coordination
  • Cost benchmarking against market rates
  • Waste reduction and materials optimisation
  • Project schedule monitoring and recovery planning

Renovations

Many existing buildings in Ghana have ageing structural elements, inadequate drainage, or layouts that no longer meet the needs of their occupants. KCE Dynamics provides structured renovation services — from initial structural assessment through redesign, supervised refurbishment, and documented handover.

We don't patch problems. We identify root causes, design appropriate solutions, and manage the refurbishment to the same standard as new construction — with quality control on site throughout.

  • Structural condition assessment and inspection reports
  • Renovation design and construction drawings
  • Supervised refurbishment with quality control
  • Drainage, roofing, and waterproofing improvements
  • Layout and functionality upgrades
  • Final inspection and handover documentation

Requote & Estimation

Too many construction projects in Ghana start with contractor quotes that are either undercosted — leading to mid-build cost escalations — or grossly inflated, leaving clients no basis to evaluate them. KCE Dynamics provides independent, data-driven cost estimates and requotes you can rely on.

Whether you're entering a project, questioning a quote you've received, or need a formal requote mid-build after scope changes, we produce detailed quantity take-offs and unit rate analyses based on current Ghanaian market rates.

  • Detailed bill of quantities and quantity take-off
  • Unit rate analysis based on current market rates
  • Independent review of contractor quotations
  • Cost benchmarking and value engineering
  • Mid-project requotes following scope changes
  • Written estimation report for client or funder review
